StillHer Foundation

The Strategy

Road to Her Smile is the first intervention. It will not be the last.

The Vision

A Foundation Built for Continuity

The StillHer Foundation was not created for one documentary or one woman's story. It was built to establish a sustained, repeatable model for identifying women who have been failed by institutional systems, documenting their experiences with cinematic honesty, and funding the restoration they were denied.

Road to Her Smile is the proof of concept. Lia Spriggs's story is the story that opens the door. Behind it are hundreds of women whose experiences with medical abandonment, institutional betrayal, and systemic neglect remain undocumented and unaddressed.

The Model

Four Pillars of Intervention

01

Identify

Locate women failed by medical, religious, or family institutions who have no remaining avenue for recourse or restoration on their own.

02

Document

Produce cinematic, honest documentary work that tells each story with dignity and without sensationalism. Let the facts carry the weight.

03

Fund

Raise targeted funding for the specific restoration each woman needs, whether medical, legal, or structural. Every dollar is accountable.

04

Advocate

Use each documentary as a vehicle for systemic accountability. Press, legal channels, and public awareness create the pressure that prevents repetition.

Beyond This Film

An Ongoing Series of Interventions

The Foundation's intent is for Road to Her Smile to establish the model, then to apply that model to other women in similar circumstances. Each subsequent project will follow the same four-pillar approach: identify, document, fund, and advocate.

The stories exist. The women exist. What has been missing is the infrastructure to find them, tell their stories with the care they deserve, and convert that storytelling into tangible restoration. That is what StillHer Foundation builds.
