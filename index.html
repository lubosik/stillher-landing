Her Story
"Leah Spriggs was not always the woman the world would later overlook."

Before the silence. Before the suffering. Before the system failed her, she was a woman of presence. Born in Verona, Italy, she carried a natural elegance that luxury brands spend millions trying to replicate. At the height of her early life, Leah was a model. She stood in rooms where aesthetics mattered, where presence was currency.

Then she met a man on a military post in Vicenza. He promised her love, America, a white picket fence. She left everything: her country, her language, her familiarity. And followed him across the ocean. Over the course of their twelve-year marriage, she discovered he had been unfaithful for nearly the entirety of it.

Her Road

1

Born in Verona, Italy

A woman of natural elegance. A future that looked expansive. She modelled. She stood in rooms where presence was currency and beauty opened doors.

2

She Met a Man in Uniform

Vicenza military post. A promise of love, America, a white picket fence. She believed him, as people believe those who speak with certainty.

3

She Left Everything Behind

Crossed an ocean. Left her country, her language, her familiarity. Built a life from scratch in a country that was never quite hers. A twelve-year marriage.

4

Betrayal, Layered and Relentless

Infidelity spanning nearly the entirety of their marriage. A single immigrant mother. Two children. No safety net. Then an eviction notice, final.

5

A Doctor Removed 13 Teeth

Referred by her church. Trusted the process. Johns Hopkins Hospital. Thirteen teeth extracted. When she returned, the doctor had disappeared. No follow-up. No implants. Left toothless.

6

The Church: $7.2M Renovation

While Leah sat in aftermath, toothless, sick, without recourse, the church gave her a $25 grocery gift card. Days later: a $7.2 million renovation announced from the pulpit.

What We Stand For

Why This Documentary Matters

Dignity

This is about more than teeth. When systems strip a woman of her ability to eat, speak, and smile, they strip her identity. Dignity is not a luxury. It is the baseline.

Justice

A doctor disappeared. A church stayed silent. This documentary names what happened and demands accountability for the systems that failed Leah, and women like her.

Restoration

Road to Her Smile is Leah's road back. And with your support, she won't walk it alone. We believe in restoration not just of health, but of dignity, voice, and belonging.

The Foundation

"Still Her Foundation exists to restore dignity to women who have been abandoned by the systems meant to protect them."

We document, advocate, and fund the restoration of women failed by medical institutions, religious organizations, and family structures. Our work begins with Leah. It will not end with her.
